PROBLEM STATEMENT: Print subarray with maximum subarray sum (extended version of above problem)

    APPROACH:

        PYTHON CODE:

        nums=[-2,1,-3,4,-1,2,1,-5,4]
        maxSum=0
        Sum=0
        ans=[]
        for i in nums:
            Sum+=i
            ans.append(i)
            if Sum<0:
                Sum=0
                while ans:
                    ans.pop()
            elif Sum>maxSum:
                maxSum=Sum
